As a supplier, you are the backbone of the stores' inventory.

## 1. Catalog Control
-   Manage your **Productos** (Supplier Products).
-   Define wholesale prices and bulk units.
-   Keep your availability up to date so stores know what they can order.

## 2. Fulfillment Flow
Incoming requests from stores are in **Pedidos**.
1.  **Accept**: Confirms you have the stock and the order is in your queue.
2.  **Ship**: Mark as **En Ruta** when the delivery vehicle departs.
3.  **Delivery**: provide your delivery verification to the store owner to finalize the transaction.

## 3. Relationship with Companies
If you are linked to a **Company** (Brand), your products will be highlighted as official brand items, increasing trust for store owners.
