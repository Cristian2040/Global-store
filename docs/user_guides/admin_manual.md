The Control Panel provides the oversight needed to run the GlobalStore ecosystem.

## 1. Dashboard Overview
Your home screen displays live platform metrics:
-   **Total Sales**: The health of the ecosystem.
-   **Commissions**: Your estimated earnings.
-   **Activity**: New businesses and users joining the platform.

## 2. Business Approvals
You control who participates:
-   **Suppliers**: Approve or Reject new supplier applications.
-   **Stores**: Monitor store activity and suspension if necessary.
-   **Users**: Detailed audit of all registered accounts.

## 3. Data Governance
-   **Global Catalog**: Ensure images and names for products are high-quality.
-   **System Settings**: Periodically review and update platform tax and commission rates to stay competitive.
