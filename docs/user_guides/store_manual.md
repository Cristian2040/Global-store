Managing your retail business on GlobalStore is simple and automated.

## 1. Inventory Management
Your **Inventario** is where you control your stock.
-   **Adding Products**: Use "Agregar Producto" to search the Global Catalog and add it to your store.
-   **Pricing**: You set your own prices.
-   **Stock**: Stock levels decrease automatically when customers buy from you.

## 2. Restocking from Suppliers
If you run out of stock:
1.  Go to **Proveedores**.
2.  Select a supplier and the products you need.
3.  Submit a **Restock Order**.
4.  **Confirm Delivery**: When the supplier arrives, use the private code to confirm receipt and instantly update your stock.
